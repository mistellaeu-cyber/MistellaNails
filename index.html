Application Guide

How to Apply

1

Prep Your Nails

Remove any old polish. Push back cuticles gently, then buff the surface of your natural nails lightly. Wipe with a lint-free pad and rubbing alcohol to remove all oils — this is the most important step for long wear.

2

Size & Match

Lay out all press-ons and match each one to your nail. The press-on should cover your nail from side to side without touching skin.

3

Apply Adhesive

Use nail glue for a firm, longer-lasting hold (up to 2 weeks), or the included nail tabs for an easy, damage-free wear of 1–3 days. Apply to the natural nail, not the press-on.

4

Press & Hold

Place the press-on at the cuticle line and press down firmly for 30 seconds. Avoid water for at least 1 hour after application.

5

Removal

Soak in warm soapy water for 5–10 minutes, then gently wiggle the press-on off. Never force or peel — your sets are reusable if removed properly. Store in their original box.

Tips for Longer Wear

✦ Avoid prolonged water exposure and harsh cleaning products — wear gloves when washing dishes.
✦ Don't use your nails as tools — opening cans or pulling tabs will lift the edges.
✦ Apply a thin top coat over the edge of the press-on and your natural nail for extra seal.
✦ If an edge lifts slightly, press it back down with a tiny drop of nail glue immediately.
✦ For special events, apply the evening before — adhesive bonds stronger after a few hours.
✦ Your sets are reusable 3–5 times if removed gently and stored flat in their box.

Measure in 3 Steps

Accurate measurements mean a perfect fit — please measure all 10 nails before ordering. When in between sizes, I recommend sizing up for comfort.

  1. Cut a thin strip of paper or use a flexible tape measure.
  2. Wrap it around the widest part of your nail from one side wall to the other — don't include the skin on either side.
  3. Mark where it meets, then measure the length with a ruler in millimetres. Note all 10 fingers (both hands).

Not sure? Send me a photo of your hand alongside a ruler and I'll size you myself — no extra charge.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my press-ons last?+
With nail glue and proper prep (no oils on the nail), sets last 1–2 weeks. With adhesive tabs, expect 1–3 days. Avoiding water and using your nails gently extends wear significantly.
Will press-ons damage my natural nails?+
No — if applied and removed correctly. Always soak off gently in warm water; never peel or force them off. The press-ons themselves do not damage nails; only improper removal can cause minor surface wear.
Are the sets reusable?+
Yes! With gentle removal and proper storage, sets can be worn 3–5 times. Store them flat in their original box after each use.
How long does a custom order take?+
Custom designs typically take 2–3 weeks from the time I receive your measurements and confirm the design. For urgent needs (weddings, events), contact me as early as possible.
Where do you ship?+
I ship across Europe. Delivery within Croatia typically takes 2–4 business days; orders to other European countries usually arrive in 5–10 business days. Contact me for a shipping quote to your country.
How do I send my nail measurements?+
Use the order form below or DM me on Instagram. I'll send you a simple sizing guide. You can also send a photo of your hand next to a ruler and I'll measure for you.
Can I request a design I saw on Instagram?+
Absolutely — send me the reference image and I'll tell you if it's achievable and what the timeline looks like. I welcome inspiration from any source.
What's the first-order discount?+
New clients receive 10% off their first order. Just mention it when you get in touch and I'll apply it to your quote.
